Brimstone

ControllerEasy

Brimstone is the most consistent controller in Valorant Mobile. His smokes are instant (no cast animation), his stim beacon is team-wide, and his orbital strike is the most reliable damage ult in the game.

Why Brimstone on Mobile?

  • Instant smokes — Unlike Omen, Brimstone's smokes deploy immediately. No timing window to exploit
  • Team stim — Stim Beacon gives +speed and +damage to everyone in radius. Massive on attack
  • Reliable ultimate — Orbital Strike is point-and-click, travels in a straight line. No skill shot guessing
  • Easy to use — Low mechanical demand. Position well and press buttons at right times

Skills on Mobile

Stim Beacon (C)

Place it at your feet or teammate locations. 8% damage boost + speed. On attack, stim before pushing.

Incendiary (Q)

Damage field. Block defuse or flush enemies from behind cover. On mobile, tap-aim quickly.

Smoke (E) x3

Best smokes in game — instant deployment, long duration, large area. Assign all 3 to accessible slots.

Orbital Strike (X)

Damage over time in an area. Use it on plant spots, choke points, or to deny defuse. Most reliable ult on mobile.

Mobile Tips

  • Smoke pre-planning — On mobile, you can't smoke while moving. Plan your smoke line before pushing
  • Smoke placement — Use smokes to block sniper angles first, then chokepoints
  • Stim beacon priority — Give it to duelists pushing first, or yourself for trading power
  • Strike on plant — The most impactful use: strike enemy spawn when allies are planting

When to Pick Brimstone

Best controller for beginners. He's the safe pick on any map where you need reliable smokes and some team utility. He's slightly weaker than Omen on maps requiring teleport, but his ult is significantly more reliable on mobile.
