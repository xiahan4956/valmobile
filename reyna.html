Reyna

DuelistMedium Difficulty

Reyna is the feast-or-famine duelist. She thrives on confidence and punish overextensions brutally. On mobile, her self-sustain makes her forgiving for aggressive players who can land headshots.

Why Reyna on Mobile?

  • Self-sustain — Devour and Dismiss heal her on kills. No team dependency for sustain
  • Dismiss immunity — Invincibility frames during Dismiss. Use it to escape with the bomb
  • Flash + trade — Her flash is instant and self-targeted. Perfect for aggressive peeks
  • High skill ceiling — Rewarding for players with good aim

Skills on Mobile

Leer (C)

Eye that flashes enemies in line of sight. Self-cast on mobile means you can flash while reloading — assign to auto-fire.

Devour (Q)

Consume Soul Orb for instant heal. On mobile, use it immediately after getting a kill — don't wait.

Dismiss (E)

Invincibility + reposition. Use it to escape after getting a pick, or to plant with impunity.

Empress (X)

Auto-reload + infinite spirit. On mobile, use it when you have a clear firing angle — you're a headshot machine.

Mobile Tips

  • Leer timing — Throw Leer, peek immediately while enemies are blind. Your headshot wins the duel.
  • Devour immediately — Health advantage matters most in the next fight. Don't save it.
  • Dismiss to plant — One of the best plant tricks: Dismiss through site with the spike for a free plant.
  • Not for passive players — Reyna needs aggression. If you're playing passive, Jett is better.

When to Pick Reyna

Reyna is best when you're confident and have good aim. She's a pub-stomper on mobile — dominates uncoordinated teams but struggles against coordinated stacks who trade properly. Pick her to climb through solo-queue quickly.
