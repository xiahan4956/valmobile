Sage

SentinelEasy

Sage is the backbone of most Valorant Mobile teams. Her sustain and utility make her forgiving for newer mobile players, and devastating in coordinated hands. She's the best agent for solo-queue climbing on mobile.

Why Sage on Mobile?

  • Self-heal — Slow yourself + heal = long survive time without relying on team
  • Forgiving kit — Wall blocks rounds even if placed imperfectly
  • Clutch potential — Resurrection is the most impactful ultimate in the game
  • Team dependency — Works well even with randoms

Skills on Mobile

Slow Orb (C)

Plant at chokepoints. On mobile, pre-aim placement before enemies push.

Healing Orb (Q)

Prioritize teammates who are trading, not the one who's safe. Heal yourself when low — don't save it.

Barrier Orb (E)

Block chokepoints or save teammates being pushed. On mobile, place it fast — hesitation costs rounds.

Resurrection (X)

Only use when you have safe information. On mobile, ping the revive location to your team before casting.

Mobile Tips

  • Healing priority — Duelists first, then sentinels. Never let a full-health teammate waste your heal
  • Wall placement — Use Sage wall offensively on attack (site takes) and defensively on retake
  • Positioning — Stay back, you're the most valuable asset. Don't peek aggressively
  • Slow usage — Slow orb yourself when crossing open areas — enemies struggle to hit you while slowed

When to Pick Sage

Always a safe choice. She's strongest on defense but works on attack too. If you're solo-queueing, she's your best bet for climbing because she compensates for bad teammates.
