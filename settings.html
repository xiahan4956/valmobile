Best Settings for Valorant Mobile

Recommended sensitivity, HUD layout, and control configurations.

🎯 General Sensitivity

Universal Sensitivity

DPI (DPI Scaling)400
In-Game Sensitivity0.35 – 0.50
Swipe Sensitivity0.8 – 1.0
Aim AssistON (recommended)
Auto-Vertical SensitivityON
💡 Start conservative: If you're coming from PC Valorant, start at 0.35 sensitivity and work up. Mobile aim is different — precision matters more than speed.

🖥️ HUD Layout Recommendations

The default HUD works, but these tweaks improve play:

HUD Optimization

Ability ButtonsEnlarge + move closer to thumbs
Fire ButtonRight side, lower position
Movement JoystickLeft side, slightly lower than default
ADS ButtonRight thumb area, easy to reach
Map ButtonTop-right corner, away from play area

🕹️ Per-Agent Sensitivity

Duelists

0.45–0.55 (higher = better flick)

Controllers

0.35–0.45 (precision over speed)

Sentinels

0.35–0.40 (anchored playstyle)

Initiators

0.38–0.45 (balanced)

📌 Other Tips

  • 4-Finger vs 6-Finger: 4-finger is standard. 6-finger adds two extra ability buttons but requires practice
  • Gyro Aiming: If available on your device, gyro adds precision at range. Enable in settings
  • Audio: Use headphones. Footstep audio is critical in Valorant and works identically on mobile
  • Performance Mode: Enable if your device heats up. Slight visual downgrade for smoother FPS
