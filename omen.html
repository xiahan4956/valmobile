Omen

ControllerMedium Difficulty

Omen is the mobile-friendly controller with teleport mobility. While his smokes require timing (cast animation), his paranoid shield and blind give him strong defensive tools that work on any screen size.

Why Omen on Mobile?

  • Teleport — Reposition instantly. Get off bad angles, fake plants, or escape post-death
  • Map coverage — His smokes cover large areas and can be placed from safety
  • Versatility — Both attack and defense strong kit, works on any map
  • Paranoia — Point-and-click flash is one of the easiest abilities to use on mobile

Skills on Mobile

Paranoia (C)

Instant blind projectile. Easiest flash on mobile — fires in a straight line. Use it to peek aggressively.

Dark Cover (Q)

Smoke with cast delay. On mobile, pre-cast before peeking to have it ready instantly.

Shrouded Step (E)

Teleport up to 15m. On mobile, plan your teleport position before casting — no teleport while blinded.

From the Shadows (X)

Become invisible and teleport to any location. On mobile, exit far from enemies — your teleport endpoint is visible to them.

Mobile Tips

  • Teleport uses — Fake a teleport to bait enemies, then don't commit. Or teleport after getting first pick.
  • Smoke timing — Dark Cover has ~1s delay before deploying. Account for this in fights.
  • Ult escape — Use ult to escape bad retake situations. Always have an exit plan.
  • Flash before peek — Paranoia + peek is your strongest combo. Flash, swing, shoot.

When to Pick Omen

Best controller on maps with vertical space (Bind, Haven) where teleport provides unmatched rotation speed. He's harder to master than Brimstone but has higher ceiling on mobile due to teleport utility.
