Sova

InitiatorMedium Difficulty

Sova is the premier information gatherer in Valorant Mobile. His recon bolts and drone provide unmatched map control intelligence, making him indispensable in coordinated play.

Why Sova on Mobile?

  • Information is king — Knowing where enemies aren't lets you rotate aggressively
  • Drone survivability — Controlled drone burns time and reveals without risking yourself
  • Post-plant denial — Shock Darts block defuse and force retake decisions
  • Screens to sound — Recon bolt screen-taps give audio cues even without direct line of sight

Skills on Mobile

Recon Bolt (C)

Pins enemies and reveals them for 3s. On mobile, pre-fire it into common angles. The sound ping is crucial intel.

Shock Darts (Q)

Long-range damage. Use them to finish low-health enemies or deny plant. On mobile, aim carefully — they drop significantly.

Drone (E)

Your most valuable tool. Move it slowly through chokepoints. On mobile, use 2-finger control for precise movement.

Hunter's Fury (X)

3-line ultimate that pierces walls. On mobile, pre-aim at sound cues — it travels fast and hits through most surfaces.

Mobile Tips

  • Drone control — Use 2 thumbs: one for movement joystick, one for forward/back. This gives precision control
  • Arrow durability — Arrows can be reused if they don't hit anything. Check your arrow count before buying
  • Recon bolt spots — Pre-place bolts at common defensive positions, not where you're actively fighting
  • Ultimate tracking — Start charging ult on defense, fire it when 2+ enemies are clustered at a choke

When to Pick Sova

Best in coordinated teams where drone info translates to rotates and trades. On mobile solo-queue, his value drops if teammates don't use your intel. Pick him when you have voice comms.
