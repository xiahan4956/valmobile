Jett

Duelist Medium Difficulty

Jett is the most mobile agent in Valorant Mobile, built for fragging and fragging alone. On mobile, her agility is even more pronounced — skilled thumb control can make her near-untouchable.

Why Jett on Mobile?

  • Highest mobility — Tailwind dash is instant on mobile with well-placed buttons
  • Self-sustain — Cloudburst smoke gives instant cover without relying on team
  • Fragging potential — Blade Storm is devastating with good aim on touch controls
  • Escape tools — Updraft + dash = near-instant off-angle escapes

Skills on Mobile

Cloudburst (C) — Smoke

8 charges. Instant cast — throw it where you're fighting, not where you plan to run. Assign to right thumb for quick access.

Updraft (Q) — Mobility

Vertical boost. On mobile, tap-drag UP immediately after casting for maximum height. Essential for attacking Heaven angles.

Tailwind (E) — Signature Dash

Your core tool. Dash-to-fight corners. Never dash backward — it loses trades. Map to an easily accessible spot.

Blade Storm (X) — Ultimate

Stack kills when you have info. On mobile, pre-aim corners with Blade Storm ready for instant follow-up frags.

Mobile-Specific Tips

  • Custom button layout is critical — Put Dash and Updraft on your right thumb for fast reactions
  • Sensitivity matters most — High sens (0.4-0.5 in-game) lets you 180° for retreating threats
  • Smoke placement — Don't save Cloudburst. Use it aggressively during fights
  • Drift compensation — Mobile momentum feels heavier than PC. Account for it when dashing through angles
  • Attack default angles first — Jett's mobility lets you take risks PC players can't

⚙️ Recommended Settings

When to Pick Jett

Jett excels when you need a primary fragger. Pick her on attack when you want to entry aggressively, or on defense when you can drone-play with Sage first. She's weaker on tight maps where Omen's teleport offers more utility.
