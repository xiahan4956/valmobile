Killjoy

SentinelEasy

Killjoy is the queen of site defense in Valorant Mobile. Her turret + alarmbot combo locks down flanks automatically, making her perfect for players who want strong impact without constant mechanical focus.

Why Killjoy on Mobile?

  • Automatic flank watch — Turret + bot cover two angles while you focus on main
  • Post-plant dominance — Nanoswarm denies defuse. Force the enemy to shoot it or die
  • Easy to use — Place abilities, then frag. No active timing required
  • Economy friendly — Her abilities are cheap. Great on tight budgets

Skills on Mobile

Alarmbot (C)

Deploys and hunts enemies in range. Triggers and slows. On mobile, place it on common flank routes.

Turret (Q)

Automatic gun that tracks enemies. Place it to watch angles you're not looking at.

Nanoswarm (E)

Grenade that deploys a damage field. On mobile, pre-throw near plant spots for post-plant denial.

Lockdown (X)

Ultimate that deploys a slowing field. Enemies inside are rooted. Use it on retake or site take.

Mobile Tips

  • UTV placement — Killjoy turret + alarmbot together = kill threat on flankers. They can't push the flank unguarded
  • Nanoswarm on plant — Throw it where enemies will plant, not where they're standing
  • Retake tool — Save lockdown for retake. The rooting effect gives your team time to swing
  • Don't over-rotate — Your utility covers flanks. Stay on site and trust your bots

When to Pick Killjoy

Best sentinel for site lockdown. She's slightly weaker on attack (turret/alarmbot are less useful when pushing), but on defense she's top-tier. Pair her with Sage for maximum sustain on site holds.
